TD

Tosin Dada

Tosin Dada is the visionary founder of Carnelian & Pearls IT Consulting, bringing over 13 years of deep expertise in the ServiceNow ecosystem. With a passion for digital transformation and a commitment to excellence, Tosin has built a reputation as one of Nigeria's leading ServiceNow consultants.

His journey began with a vision to bridge the gap between global enterprise needs and the exceptional technical talent available in Nigeria. Through Carnelian & Pearls IT, Tosin has successfully delivered transformative ServiceNow solutions to organizations across multiple continents.

Tosin's expertise spans across ITSM, ITOM, HRSD, CSM, and custom application development, with a particular strength in complex integrations and platform optimization. His leadership philosophy centers on empowering teams, fostering innovation, and maintaining the highest standards of service delivery.
