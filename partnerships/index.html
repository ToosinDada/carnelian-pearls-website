Partnerships & Deal Facilitation

We connect people, businesses, and markets, and we help good deals happen.

Opportunity is often a matter of the right introduction at the right time. We make those introductions, source what businesses need, and broker partnerships that create value on both sides.

What we do

  • Business introductions. Connecting decision-makers who should be talking.
  • Supplier sourcing. Finding and vetting suppliers for specific commercial needs.
  • Commercial matchmaking. Pairing products, services, and demand across markets.
  • Export opportunities. Opening routes for goods and services between Nigeria and international markets.
  • Strategic partnerships. Structuring longer-term relationships between organisations with aligned interests.

Why work with us

We sit between markets. Rooted in Nigeria, working internationally, we see openings that a single-market operator misses. We move on relationships, not just listings.
