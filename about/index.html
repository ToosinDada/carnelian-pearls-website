About CarnelianPearls Tech

CarnelianPearls Technologies Limited is a Nigerian technology and opportunities company. We exist to identify opportunities, build solutions, and create value, across technology, trade, and markets.

Most companies do one thing. We are built to do several, because opportunity does not respect neat categories. An opportunity reveals a product worth building. A product opens a market worth connecting. A market surfaces a partnership worth brokering. We are structured to act on all of it under one roof.

What we do

  • Partnerships and deal facilitation. We connect businesses, source suppliers, broker commercial matches, and open export and partnership opportunities, with a particular strength bridging Nigerian and international markets.
  • Venture studio. We build and launch our own software and AI products, and incubate the next ones. Taska is the first live venture.
  • Opportunities. A forming marketplace for business, supplier, export, real estate, and investment opportunities worth pursuing.

How we think

The name holds the idea. Carnelian is a stone associated with drive and decisive action. Pearls are value that forms over time and is found rather than manufactured. We pair the two on purpose: the appetite to act, and the patience to build value that lasts.

Where we are

We are based in Nigeria and we work across borders. Being rooted in one of Africa's largest markets while operating internationally is not a limitation. It is the vantage point that makes much of what we do possible.
