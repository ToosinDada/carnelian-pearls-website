Live venture

Taska

Taska is our first live venture. It is an operations platform for facilities and maintenance companies that replaces WhatsApp coordination, Excel trackers, and manual follow-up with a single tool to run jobs, assign teams, track customers and sites, and automate recurring maintenance.

Visit Taska

Inside Taska

What Taska does

One workflow tool for jobs, teams, customers, and recurring maintenance, in place of WhatsApp threads and spreadsheets. Built for facilities and maintenance teams in Nigeria and beyond.

Jobs and work orders

Run every job from request to sign off, with status, priority, notes, and before and after photos on one auditable timeline.

Customers and sites

Hold every client and all of their service locations in one place, each with its own job history.

Team assignment

Send the right technicians to the right jobs. Field staff see only the work assigned to them.

Recurring maintenance

Set a schedule once and Taska generates the recurring visits automatically, so planned maintenance never slips.

Dashboard and alerts

See what is open, overdue, and due soon at a glance, with email and in app notifications for the team.

Roles for the whole team

Secure, role based access for admins, ops managers, supervisors, and technicians, so everyone sees exactly what they should.
