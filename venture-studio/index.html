Live venture

Taska

Taska is our first live venture. It is Nigeria's trusted artisan marketplace: a mobile-first app that connects people who need work done with verified artisans near them, from carpenters and plumbers to electricians.

Visit Taska

Inside Taska

What Taska does

Finding a good artisan in Nigeria usually means asking around and hoping. Taska turns that into a marketplace, with the trades people actually need and the trust to go with them.

Verified artisans

Carpenters, plumbers, electricians, and more, verified and searchable by the people who need them.

Both sides of the job

One app, two doors. Customers join to find services. Artisans join to be found and to win work.

Near you, on your phone

Built mobile first for Nigeria, and installable straight from the browser with nothing to download from a store.
